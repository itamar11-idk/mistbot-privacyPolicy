Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 22, 2026

1. Introduction

Mistbot is a community management and utility platform designed for the Attack on Titan: Revolution (Roblox) community on Discord. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, usage, and protection of your data when interacting with Mistbot to utilize our marketplace, vouching, and safety features.

By inviting, interacting with, or otherwise utilizing Mistbot, you consent to the data practices described in this document.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Vouching & Reputation: Discord User IDs, usernames of relevant participants, carry types, and recorded success metrics.
  • Marketplace Listings: The content of trade listings, item inventories, and your associated Discord User ID.
  • Safety Reports: Evidence, context, and account details submitted during the scam reporting or blacklist appeal process.
  • Lobby Details: Temporary information regarding game lobbies you choose to host or join.
  • Assistant Interactions: Queries or direct commands sent to the integrated AI features.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Platform Identifiers: Your Discord User ID, current username, and avatar URL to facilitate user profiles and leaderboards.
  • Automated Trade Processing: Within server-designated trade channels, message content is programmatically processed by our automated systems to categorize and syndicate marketplace listings.
  • Contextual & Diagnostic Data: Discord Server (Guild) IDs to isolate configuration environments, as well as operational logs (such as timestamps and execution errors) to maintain platform stability.

2.3 Information NOT Collected

Mistbot operates on a principle of minimal data collection. Specifically, we do not:

  • Collect, process, or store passwords, login credentials, or payment information.
  • Monitor, read, or process messages outside of designated bot-interaction or explicitly configured trade channels.

3. How We Use Your Information

The data we collect is utilized strictly to provide and improve the services offered by Mistbot, including but not limited to:

  • Maintaining accurate global leaderboards and carrier verification systems.
  • Facilitating an interconnected, cross-server marketplace for users.
  • Providing automated game assistance, real-time trade categorization, and utility commands.
  • Enforcing community safety standards through a centralized, moderated blacklist.

4. Third-Party Services

To function effectively and reliably, Mistbot integrates with industry-standard third-party infrastructure. Data is only transmitted to the extent strictly necessary for the bot's features to operate:

Service Category Operational Purpose Data Transmitted
Discord Core Platform Interface Interactions, User IDs, Guild IDs, specific messages.
Secure Database Providers Persistent Data Storage Stored user statistics, active listings, and global blacklist data.
AI Processing Infrastructure Natural Language Parsing User queries and content submitted in designated trade channels.
Media Integration Services Search Functionality Anonymized search terms (e.g., keyword queries for media/GIFs).

Important Notice: By utilizing AI-powered commands or posting within designated automated trade channels, your message content is processed by our secure AI infrastructure partners in real-time to generate categorizations and responses.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Storage Framework: Persistent data is housed in secure, authenticated database environments.
  • Configuration Isolation: Server-specific configurations are stored in isolated file structures to prevent cross-contamination.
  • Security Posture: We employ industry-standard encryption and secure token authentication protocols to protect data in transit and at rest. We do not, and will never, sell your personal data.

6. Data Retention Policies

We retain data only as long as necessary to fulfill the operational purposes described above:

  • Vouches & Blacklist: Retained indefinitely to maintain the long-term integrity of the community, unless explicitly removed via an official staff appeal.
  • Marketplace Dynamics: Trade listings are automatically expunged from our active database following 6 hours of inactivity.
  • Transient Systems: Temporary data, such as lobby configurations and caching layers, are wiped immediately upon expiration or system cycle.
  • AI Interactions: Query processing occurs in real-time; we do not maintain a permanent, linked database of your specific natural language queries.

7. Global Network Features

Mistbot operates certain features on a cross-server (global) basis to maximize utility for the community:

  • Global Marketplace: Submitted trade listings may be broadcast to all external Discord servers subscribed to the Mistbot network.
  • Global Safety Standards: Blacklist status applied to an offending user is enforced globally across all participating Mistbot servers to protect the wider ecosystem.
  • Public Safety Alerts: Fully verified scam reports and relevant evidence may be distributed to community staff and public warning channels.

8. Your Rights & Contact

Under standard digital privacy principles, you maintain the right to understand and manage your data footprint within Mistbot. You may request to:

  • View the programmatic data we currently have stored associated with your Discord ID.
  • Request the deletion or resetting of specific data (such as clearing your vouch history or terminating active marketplace listings).
  • Opt-out of automated processing by refraining from using designated trade channels or commands.

For formal data requests, policy inquiries, or technical support, please join our Support Server and contact the development administration team directly.
